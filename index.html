My Story

Hey gorgeous!

Chances are if you’ve found my website it’s because you are looking for the mermaid hair of your dreams. You are in the right spot! Before we dive into the world of extensions let me introduce myself. My name is Sarah Gonzalez and I’m a passionate hairstylist based out of Cape Coral, FL. I’ve lived in the sunshine state for over 2 decades and I am in love with the tropical weather and beaches.

When I’m not in the salon you can bet I’m spending time with my family somewhere in the sun or catching up on the latest IBE®️ training, to further my expertise.

When stepping into the studio, you can expect to be greeted with a warm smile and a relaxing, pampered experience. You will leave feeling refreshed with an added boost in confidence.

You’ll find that I am very thorough with my consultations to ensure the best results for your hair expectations and dreams. I will always ask what you loved and what you’d like to see different at each appointment! Worried about styling and care? Don’t you worry! Instead of having gorgeous hair only on the day you sit in my chair, I’ll set you up for weeks of success in between visits with my styling training and coaching.

I can not wait to meet you! - Sarah
