Extensions

Bead Extensions are the safest method for weft extensions on the market, and my preferred method. Style your hair any way you want! The key to this method is having both a tension and irritation free application. I.B.E. consists of applying beads to your section of hair, then applying handtied wefts above and below to create a completely seamless application. You can add volume, length or both to your hair and almost everyone is a candidate.

Hair Care

I.B.E. does require maintenance of 6-8+ weeks depending on the person. We do not do express move-ups like other methods do, but rather reinstall them fresh. Hair will need to be replaced every 9 months.

Follow this guide below to get the most out of your hair.

Good habits

    Daily

    -Brush gently. Use your free hand to secure the base of the weft.

    -Use a scrunchy to secure your ponytails or buns

    -Use a wet brush or boar bristle brush.

    -Sleep in a low pony or braid. Use a silk pillow or head scarf.

    -Use wet/dry oil (clear only).

    -Always use a heat protectant

    -follow this link for more tips

    Washing

    -Deep condition weekly. Bi-weekly if possible.

    -Brush through the ends before shampooing.

    -Use salon grade sulfate free shampoo/conditioner.

    -Shampooing in the shower is best/easiest. RINSE, RINSE, RINSE.

    -No conditioner at top of the weft (can cause slipping).

    -follow this link for more tips

Bad habits

    Never

    -Don't be rough while brushing or any other activity.

    -Never brush the baseline of the wefts, brush right under.

    -Avoid chlorinated/salt water.

    -If you will get your extensions wet dampen w/tap water first. Apply leave in conditioner, then put your extensions into 2 tight braids to secure. Wash after to best maintain your extensions.

    -Use salon grade sulfate free shampoo/conditioner.

    -No sunscreens with "Avobezone" as an ingredient. Your hair will turn pink/coral. Most mineral sunscreens are safe.

    -Avoid over use of purple/blue shampoos.

    -Do not go to sleep with wet hair. Always keep dry.

    -No fast drying sprays and dry shampoo.

    -Avoid protein fill products.

    -Avoid spraying hair spray or dry texture spray before heat styling.

    -Do not scrub w/ nails on weft rows, use the pads of finger tips.

    -NEVER HAVE BLEACH APPLIED TO YOUR EXTENSIONS.
